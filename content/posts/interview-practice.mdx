## Technical Interviews are hard, here are some practice to process easier:

### Websites 
* [LeeCode](https://leetcode.com/explore/interview/card/top-interview-questions-easy/) - Highly recommend, questions are from real interviews and well written

* [GlassDoor](https://www.glassdoor.com) - You can look up a company that you are interviewing with and look at their previous interview questions.

* [HackerRank](https://www.hackerrank.com/interview/interview-preparation-kit) - Questions are not always well worded but a lot of companies use HackerRank for their interviews.
